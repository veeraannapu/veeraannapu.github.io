01. About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Software Engineer with a Master's in Computer Science from Columbus State University and a track record of building systems that handle real scale. I gravitate toward the hard problems — distributed data flows, event-driven architecture, and backend services that need to be both reliable and fast.

At Grainger, I work across the order management domain, building everything from Kafka-driven data pipelines in Python to backend services in Java and Kotlin. I take ownership across the full lifecycle — design, implementation, testing, deployment, and production support — and I actively contribute to raising engineering standards on my team.

I care about writing code that's easy to reason about, systems that are observable in production, and teams that communicate well. I enjoy mentoring, architecture discussions, and the kind of cross-functional collaboration that makes a product genuinely better.
