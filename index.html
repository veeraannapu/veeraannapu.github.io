01. About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Software Engineer with a Master's in Computer Science from Columbus State University and 8+ years building systems that handle real scale. I gravitate toward the hard problems — distributed data flows, event-driven architecture, and backend services that need to be both reliable and fast, shipped end-to-end in fast-paced Agile teams.

At Grainger, I work on Customer Order — making orders searchable via HTTP APIs, streaming order events through Kafka, and curating order data across the ecosystem using Spring Boot/Kotlin microservices and Databricks/Snowflake. Before that I built healthcare platforms at GuideWell Florida Blue and insurance-domain applications at Aflac.

I care about writing code that's easy to reason about, systems that are observable in production, and teams that communicate well. I enjoy mentoring, architecture discussions, and the kind of cross-functional collaboration that makes a product genuinely better.
